Data Deletion Instructions

Our application respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data.

If you wish to request deletion of your personal data associated with our application, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to: info@betna.com
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include your Facebook account email or user ID used with our app

Once we receive your request, we will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable laws.
